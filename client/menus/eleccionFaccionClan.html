Clans with a royal alignment only admit members of Banderbil's Royal Army. They will fight each and every criminal in these lands under the King and Banderbill's Council. PCs will be automatically expelled if they cease being members of the Royal Army.

Clans with a lawful alignment can include both members of the Royal Army and private citizens within their ranks. PCs will be automatically expelled if they become criminals.

Neutral clans have no specific alignment. Only criminals and citizens which are not part of any faction will be accepted. PCs will be automatically expelled if they join any faction.

Criminal clans are integrated exclusively by criminals. Members of the Dark Legion may also join these clans. PCs will be automatically expelled if they cease being criminals.

These clans are integrated exclusively by members of the Dark Legion, under the orders of the Dark Lord and his subjects. PCs will be automatically expelled if they cease being members of the Dark Legion.
